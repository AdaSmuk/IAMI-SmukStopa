Main workshop Katowice, Poland

This is the workshop where it all started.
Back in 2014 we decided that our life have to take a big turn, so we can finally start making our dreams come true.
We turned an old cabin at the back of my parents house into a workshop, in which we created our first and most popular hair pin design.
Right now it's bigger and better, with a small warehouse where we store and prepare all types of leathers.
It's also a place where most of your orders are created.

 International workshop Birmingham, England

Our second workshop, founded after our student moved to England.
We wanted to keep in touch and expand our business together so we bought a small warehouse to turn it into another workshop and shipping center.
If you live over sea or in any country to which shipping is better from England your orders first fly right there.
If anything is not right when your order arrives in England, we can repair it or even make a new one.
